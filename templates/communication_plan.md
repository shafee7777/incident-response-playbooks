## Purpose
Define how internal and external communication is handled during security incidents.

## Stakeholders
- Executive leadership
- SOC / IR team
- Legal & Compliance
- IT Operations
- Public Relations

## Severity Levels
| Severity | Description | Communication Requirement |
|---------|-------------|----------------------------|
| SEV-1 | Major outage/data breach | Notify all stakeholders immediately |
| SEV-2 | Significant service impact | Notify leadership + IT | 
| SEV-3 | Minor localized issue | SOC/IR only |

## Communication Channels
- Email
- Slack/Teams
- Phone bridge
- Ticketing system
- Incident conference room
